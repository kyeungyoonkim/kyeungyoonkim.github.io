College Tuition Analysis

Mar 2024

This project analyzed the relationships between tuition, income levels, racial diversity, and early/mid-career pay across universities using Tableau. Key insights included:

  • High tuition often correlates with higher early career pay but not necessarily long-term ROI.
  • Racial and socioeconomic diversity factors impact student outcomes.
  • Interactive dashboards allowed dynamic filtering by state, income, and program type.

Skills Applied: Tableau, Data Analysis, Visualization, Statistical Interpretation
