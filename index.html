About Yoon Kim

I am currently working as a Manufacturing Quality Engineer at Qcells while pursuing my passion for data science, AI, and human-computer interaction (HCI). My research and professional interests focus on AI for accessibility and assistive technology, inclusive technology design, and leveraging machine learning to solve real-world problems.

I hold a BS in Applied Statistics from Purdue University (Dec 2024) and earned an Applications in Data Science Undergraduate Certificate. I combine statistical rigor with programming expertise in Python, R, SQL, and AI frameworks to drive data-driven solutions and insights.

My ultimate goal is to advance innovation in inclusive technology and accessible AI solutions, whether through research, applied machine learning projects, or interdisciplinary collaborations.

Outside of work, I enjoy traveling and watching MLB games, which offer creative outlets and broaden my perspective.
